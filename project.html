NASA Data for Sustainable Education

We are developing a lesson plan that incorporates NASA data to educate students about the SDGs.

Key Features

  • Interactive learning modules that engage students with hands-on activities.
  • Real-time data integration from NASA's resources for a practical learning experience.
  • Assessment tools for educators to track student progress and engagement.

Current Projects

  • Climate Change Awareness: A project aimed at educating students about the impact of climate change using real data.
  • Water Quality Monitoring: Engaging students in water quality assessments using scientific data.
  • Sustainable Agriculture Practices: Teaching students the importance of sustainable farming through interactive learning.
